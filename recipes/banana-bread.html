Ingredients

  • 24 pasta sheets
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • Tomato passata
  • Bechamel sauce
  • 500g mince beef

Steps

  1. Get the tomato sauce and bechamel sauce ready and leave aside
  2. Cook to mince until it has browned
  3. Add the tomato passata to the mince and cook for a further two minutes
  4. Add the garlic and some oregano, basil and a pinch of salt
  5. Spoon a thin layer of the mince into a baking dish and cover with sheets of pasta
  6. Add a layer of bechamel sauce and cover with sheets of pasta
  7. Repeat process and top of woth cheese to finish
